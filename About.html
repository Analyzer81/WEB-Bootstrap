How it started


Trio Kitchen’s opened on Thanksgiving Day 1990. Chef / Owner Ron Silver began making pasta and selling them to restaurants and his neighbors out of a small kitchen. Today, Great Britain’s beloved restaurant and pie shop celebrates 10 years of classic, made from scratch. Trio Kitchen was born out of love and respect, met with a desire to bring quality ingredients to the table. Simply put, we’re here to bring you a food experience you can feel good about.

Delight in Exceptional Dishes


Head Chef Tom Fraser’s menu features a selection of seafood classics inspired by Scott’s Mayfair’s original recipes, alongside specialties that pay homage to Scott’s Richmond’s riverside location. Our expansive wine list boasts an impressive collection of fine wines and vintage Champagnes, while our expert mixologists have curated a selection of unique cocktails for guests to savour alongside something special.

Live Entertainment Every Friday and Saturday


Join us and experience evenings of live acustic gitar on Fridays, and DJ on Saturdays from 8pm – 1am, alongside our exceptional menu and delectable themed cocktails.
